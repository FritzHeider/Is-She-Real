Frequently asked

Answers crafted after comparing messaging from industry leaders.

How accurate is the confidence score?

Scores reflect weighted heuristics similar to what we observed on Hive and Reality Defender dashboards. They are directional, so always pair them with manual review.

Where do image files go?

Images stay in-browser until you enable your API endpoint. Once connected, files post directly to your server—never ours.

Can I schedule recurring checks?

Yes via the API. The export format mirrors our UI so you can automate alerts or send reports to case management systems.

Do you store social lookups?

No. Results render locally and you can clear them anytime. Add API authentication to align with your compliance policies.
