Data Deletion Instructions

Updated June 5, 2024. Follow these steps to remove Is She Real account data, workspace activity, and stored media.

Self-service deletion

  1. Sign in to your workspace and open Settings → Storage.
  2. Use the Clear exports action to remove locally cached reports and downloaded JSON summaries.
  3. If you connected your own API endpoint, disable it to prevent future uploads from being forwarded.

Requesting full removal

To delete account information, billing records, and audit logs, submit a request using one of the channels below. Include the workspace slug, the email address of the requester, and whether you need confirmation for compliance filings.

  • Email: privacy@isshereal.com
  • Support portal: support.isshereal.com
  • Secure API: POST https://api.isshereal.com/v1/data-deletion with your admin token

Processing timeline

We acknowledge requests within 48 hours. Production data is removed from active systems within 7 days and from encrypted backups within 30 days, unless retention is required by law or to resolve an active dispute.

Proof of deletion

After processing we provide a signed confirmation listing the data categories removed and the timestamp of completion. You can opt in to receive automated webhook notifications at /compliance/webhooks for future requests.

Questions

Contact privacy@isshereal.com if you have questions about the status of a request or need to pause deletion for legal reasons.
